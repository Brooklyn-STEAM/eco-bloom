Event: collect trash on the beach.


This summer, join Eco Bloom for Beach Blitz: Clean and Create! - a fun, free, and impactful event for kids and teens who care about the planet. Participants will clean up a local beach, learn about marine conservation, and transform collected (safe) materials into one-of-a-kind eco-art pieces. It's the perfect way to make a difference and get creative under the sun!

Activities Include:

  • Beach Cleanup Challenge Team up to collect and sort litter and recyclables while competing in a friendly, eco-powered challenge.
  • Mini Marine Talk! Take a break and dive into a fascinating, kid-friendly discussion about ocean life, pollution, and how we can all play a part in protecting marine ecosystems
  • Trash-to-Art Workshop! Get creative as you turn clean, collected materials into colorful sculptures, collages, and other eco-inspired artwork. You’ll learn how art can be a powerful voice for the environment.
  • Pop-Up Gallery Exhibit! Share your masterpiece with the world in our beachside gallery! Friends, family, and passersby will be invited to admire your work and be inspired to care for our oceans too.

    • Event: Trash to Treasure: Eco Art


    Get ready for an inspiring, hands-on eco-art adventure! At Trash to Treasure, young campers will turn everyday trash into imaginative masterpieces. By using clean recyclables and discarded items, kids will discover how creativity and environmental responsibility go hand in hand.

    From bottle cap mosaics to cardboard castles, there’s no limit to what they can create! Each project is a chance to learn, express, and make a difference—all while having a blast.
