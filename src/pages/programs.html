Description

This summer, join Eco Bloom for Beach Blitz: Clean and Create! - a fun, free, and
impactful event for kids and teens who care about the planet. Participants will clean up a
local beach, learn about marine conservation, and transform collected (safe) materials into
one-of-a-kind eco-art pieces. It's the perfect way to make a difference and get creative
under the sun!

Activities Include:

  • Beach Cleanup Challenge - Team up to

  • Pop-Up Gallery - Show off your work in a public display and inspire others

    Event: Trash to Treasure: Eco Art
    When: Summer 2025.
    Who: Campers ages 7+

    Description

    This summer 2025, Eco Bloom will let creativity shine and sustainability take center stage!
    Trash to Treasure is a hands-on eco-art event where campers will turn everyday trash into
    imaginative masterpieces. Using clean recyclables and discarded items, kids will explore
    how art and environmental awareness go hand in hand. Whether it's building bottle cap
    mosaics or crafting cardboard castles, there's no limit to what they can create!



    Featured Activities:


  • Trash Hunt Relay - Team up and gather safe, recyclable "junk" from around camp

  • Junkyard Sculpt-Off - Create wild sculptures from found materials

  • Eco-Fashion Show - Design wearable art using only recycled items

  • Gallery Walk & Art Auction - Show off and "sell" your creations with play money


    • Why Join?

  • Celebrate creativity and self-expression

  • Learn the value of recycling and reuse

  • Have fun while making a positive impact on the planet
